How to read this chart:
  • Colored Bars: The exact volume of oil sent to each destination on a specific day.
  • Orange Line (7-Day Avg): Smooths out daily spikes to show if overall production is rising or falling.
  • Solid Black Line (Cumulative): Tracks the total oil produced so far this month (read the numbers on the right-side axis).
  • Dashed Gray Line (Ideal Path): The path you would take if every day hit the average. If the black line is above this, you are ahead of target!
  • Shaded Background Columns: These highlight weekends (Saturdays & Sundays) to help spot downtime patterns.
  • Table Colors: In the table below, Green cells are your best producing days, and Red cells are the lowest.
